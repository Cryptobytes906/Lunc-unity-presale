About LUNC Unity

LUNC Unity Lite Paper

Introduction

LUNC Unity is a community-driven token designed to empower and unify the Terra Luna Classic (LUNC) community. Our mission is to foster collaboration, resilience, and innovation within the LUNC ecosystem, ensuring a sustainable and prosperous future for all members.

Problem Statement

The Terra Luna Classic community has faced significant challenges, including market volatility and governance issues. There is a need for a unifying force that can bring the community together, drive collective action, and support the ecosystem's growth.

Solution

LUNC Unity aims to address these challenges by providing a token that:

  • Empowers Community Members: Grants voting rights and participation in governance.
  • Fosters Collaboration: Encourages community-driven initiatives and projects.
  • Supports Innovation: Funds development and technological advancements within the LUNC ecosystem.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 LUNC Unity tokens.

  • Community Rewards: 40%
  • Development Fund: 30%
  • Marketing and Partnerships: 20%
  • Team and Advisors: 10%

Use of Funds: Funds will be allocated to community projects, development, marketing, and operational expenses.

Roadmap

  • Q4 2024: Token launch and initial distribution.
  • Q1 2025: Community governance platform launch.
  • Q2 2025: Funding and support for community-driven projects.
  • Q3 2025: Expansion of partnerships and marketing efforts.
  • Q4 2025: Continuous development and ecosystem growth.
